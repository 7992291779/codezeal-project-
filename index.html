Medicine is the multidisciplinary field dedicated to safeguarding and enhancing human health. It encompasses a vast spectrum of knowledge, practices, and technologies aimed at preventing, diagnosing, and treating diseases and injuries. Medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and specialists, collaborate to provide patient-centered care. By applying scientific principles and research findings, medicine addresses both the physiological and psychological aspects of health. This field continually evolves with advancements in medical research, technology, and innovative treatments.
